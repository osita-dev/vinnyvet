About Our Practice

With over 15 years of experience, Dr. Vinny Vet provides comprehensive veterinary care and animal health services for pets, farm animals, and aquatic species. Our clinic specializes in preventive care, treatment, vaccinations, and the supply of quality veterinary drugs. We also offer expert training and consultancy services for poultry management, fish farming, and animal husbandry.
